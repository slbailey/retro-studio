Take **Gratifire** from initial idea through the first three development phases — Ideation, Pre-Production, and Prototyping — resulting in a validated playable prototype on **iOS**, with engineering aligned to **React** and **remote Mac mini** builds. Executable deliverables and GDDs you produce for the product should land in **[github.com/slbailey/gratifire](https://github.com/slbailey/gratifire)**; this **gratifire-game-studios** repo tracks the studio org and workflows only.

## Milestones

1. **Concept Lock** — Game pillars defined, core fantasy articulated, competitive positioning mapped
2. **Pre-Production Complete** — Systems decomposed, GDDs drafted for core mechanics, React/iOS architecture planned (including SSH workflow to the Mac mini)
3. **First Playable** — Prototype runs on Simulator or device from the mini’s build pipeline; findings documented; go/no-go decision made

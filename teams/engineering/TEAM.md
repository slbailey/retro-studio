The engineering department builds **Gratifire** as a **React-based iOS** application in **[github.com/slbailey/gratifire](https://github.com/slbailey/gratifire)**. The Technical Director sets architecture; the Lead Programmer runs day-to-day engineering. **react-specialist** and **ios-build-engineer** split application vs Xcode/macOS work (including **SSH** to the **Mac mini**). Gameplay, platform/runtime, AI, networking, tools, and UI programmers implement features; QA and performance analysts protect quality and budgets.

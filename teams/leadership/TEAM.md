The leadership team sets direction for RetroStudio: **ceo** resolves cross-domain escalations; **scheduling-director** owns schedule intent and EPG horizon; **playout-director** owns Playlog, channel runtime, ffmpeg, and overlays; **ingest-director** owns media prep, metadata, and availability; **systems-architect** owns MasterClock discipline and cross-cutting boundaries; **qa-director** owns contract-to-test methodology and merge-risk advisories.
